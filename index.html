WELCOME TO MY TEACHING PORTFOLIO

My name is Isabella Cattalini, and I am currently completing my Bachelor of Education (Secondary)/Bachelor of Arts (Design Innovation and Technologies) at Australian Catholic University, specialising in Textiles & Design. With a background in fashion design and a passion for creativity, I aim to inspire students through visual and hands-on learning experiences. This portfolio is a reflection of my journey as a pre-service teacher, highlighting my teaching philosophy, professional placements, and the influences that have shaped me. Here, you’ll find insights into my practice, examples of my work, and reflections on the knowledge and skills I continue to develop as I prepare for a career in education.

PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

  • Pre-service Secondary Teacher currently studying at Australian Catholic University
  • Specialising in Textiles & Design with a minor in Food Technology
  • Background in fashion design, bringing creativity and visual learning approaches into the classroom
  • Experienced in scaffolding learning, differentiation, and supporting diverse student needs through professional placements
  • Currently operate my own design and illustration business, Bellistrated, showcasing entrepreneurial and creative skills

PROFESSIONAL JOURNEY

  1. Graduated from Freeman Catholic College in 2020 with an ATAR of 80
  2. Commenced a Bachelor of Fashion & Design at the University of Technology Sydney in 2022
  3. Began Bachelor of Arts (Secondary Education) at Australian Catholic University in 2023 – current (4-year course)
  4. Completed three professional placements across Years 7–10 in TAS
